Questions

Everything you need to know about your procedure and recovery.

What happens on surgery day?
You'll arrive at our Culver City facility where our team walks you through each step. Your surgeon meets with you before the procedure, and our anesthesia team ensures your comfort throughout. Most patients go home the same day with detailed recovery instructions.
Does insurance cover procedures here?
We work with most major insurance plans. Our staff verifies your coverage before your consultation so there are no surprises. We also discuss financing options if needed.
How long is typical recovery?
Recovery varies by procedure and individual healing, but most patients return to normal activities within two to four weeks. Your surgeon creates a personalized timeline based on your specific situation.
Can I contact my surgeon after surgery?
Yes. Direct access to your surgical team is part of our concierge approach. You have a clear line of communication for questions or concerns during your recovery.
What makes outpatient surgery safer?
Our fully accredited facility maintains rigorous safety protocols and modern equipment. Outpatient procedures often mean fewer complications because you recover in a familiar environment with a personalized plan.
